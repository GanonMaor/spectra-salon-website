# Copy System — Spectra Product & Vision

> Every word on the page, defined before a line of code.
> Apple-style. Minimal. Emotionally clear. No buzzwords. No paragraphs.
> Each statement must be able to stand alone on a 5-metre screen.

---

## Voice Rules

- **Short.** Most lines under 7 words.
- **Declarative.** Statements, not pitches.
- **Concrete.** "Every gram." not "operational excellence."
- **No buzzwords.** Banned: synergy, leverage, disrupt, revolutionize, seamless, cutting-edge, next-gen, robust, holistic, empower, game-changer.
- **One idea per line.** White space does the rest.
- **Numbers are quiet.** Let the figure carry the weight; no exclamation.

## Copy Do-Not List (page-specific)

- Do not say "revolutionary," "all-in-one platform," or "end-to-end solution."
- Do not stack more than 3 lines of headline in one viewport.
- Do not pair a number with adjectives ("massive $250"). The number stands alone.
- Do not use feature bullets longer than 5 words in cinematic sections.
- Do not translate; the page is English-only by design (`Spectra Product & Vision`).
