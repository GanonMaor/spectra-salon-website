Who Buys This Intelligence

Global distributors

Distributors selling into the US and Europe need earlier demand signals: what salons consume, what is trending, and where stock should move.

Hair-care and colour brands

Brands need SKU-level consumption, formula trends, competitive share signals, and product performance insights from real salon usage.

Market-data buyers

Research firms, data marketplaces, investors, and category analysts pay for clean, aggregated market signals with geography and trend depth.

Salon operators

Owners get the immediate product value first: margin visibility, automated ordering, waste reduction, and smarter pricing decisions.

Data monetisation thesis

The same dataset that makes salons more profitable can become a second revenue engine: aggregated and de-identified benchmarks, demand signals, trend reports, and forecasting products for distributors, brands, and market-data buyers across the US, Europe, and global supply chains.

Trust principle: customer value first. Any market intelligence should be aggregated, de-identified, and permissioned; Spectra should not sell personal client data or expose identifiable salon performance.
