# Booker - Competitor Feature Research

## Company Snapshot

Booker (by Mindbody) positions itself as an all-in-one spa and salon management platform focused on booking, payments, client management, marketing automation, and AI-assisted front desk workflows.

## Core Product Areas

1. Booking and omnichannel client access
2. Integrated payments and POS
3. AI receptionist and messaging
4. Marketing and retention
5. Operations, staff management, and reporting

## Full Feature Breakdown

### 1) Booking and Client Access

- Online booking across web, mobile, and social channels
- 24/7 booking availability
- Branded booking experience positioning
- Mindbody consumer app listing support (by plan)

### 2) Payments and POS

- Integrated POS
- eCommerce and online gift card/series sales
- Checkout across front desk, website, and business app
- Payment and sales data synced into reporting

### 3) Messenger[ai] and Communication

- AI front desk workflows for missed calls and appointment responses
- SMS/webchat-based booking and client Q&A
- Centralized conversation dashboard
- One-click broadcast messaging to clients or staff

### 4) Marketing and Retention

- Automated email and text campaigns (plan-dependent depth)
- Referral and review automation on higher tiers
- Win-back and targeted retention campaigns
- Smart contact list targeting

### 5) Operations and Management

- Flexible compensation and payroll streamlining
- Inventory management
- Staff/equipment/schedule organization
- Reporting and tracking tools
- Mobile business management app
- Data migration and onboarding support

## Pricing and Packaging Signals (From Official Booker Page)

- Tiered plans: Starter, Accelerate, Ultimate, Ultimate Plus
- Clear plan expansion from core operations to advanced marketing and AI

## Competitive Strengths (From Spectra Lens)

- Broad all-in-one operations stack
- AI receptionist narrative with practical communication use cases
- Strong plan ladder for scaling teams and feature maturity
- Backed by Mindbody ecosystem and consumer discovery channels

## Opportunities for Spectra to Differentiate

- Deliver faster and cleaner UX for everyday front-desk tasks
- Reduce plan-gated friction for high-impact automations
- Provide deeper modern analytics UX with action-oriented insights
- Offer stronger salon-specific workflows with less enterprise overhead
