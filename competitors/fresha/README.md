# Fresha - Competitor Feature Research

## Company Snapshot

Fresha positions itself as a global booking and discovery platform for self-care services, connecting consumers with salons, barbers, medspas, wellness studios, and beauty professionals.

## Core Product Areas

Based on the homepage positioning, Fresha emphasizes:

1. Consumer discovery and marketplace reach
2. Instant booking and rebooking experience
3. Mobile-first client experience
4. Business-side growth exposure through platform demand
5. End-to-end client journey (book, pay, review)

## Full Feature Breakdown (Public Homepage Signals)

### 1) Discovery and Marketplace

- Search by treatment/venue, location, and time
- Strong city/category browsing model across many countries
- Large catalog of local providers and service categories
- Discovery-led growth engine for salons and beauty businesses

### 2) Booking Experience

- Instant online booking
- 24/7 booking behavior implied by customer use cases
- Appointment management flows (book, amend, cancel/reschedule)
- Fast confirmation-oriented booking UX

### 3) Mobile and Client Experience

- Dedicated mobile app focus
- Reminders mentioned by users
- Consultation forms mentioned by users
- Cross-flow convenience messaging (find, book, pay, rate in one place)

### 4) Payments and Post-Service Flow

- In-app payment convenience signals ("no cash or cards needed")
- Ratings and review loop after appointments
- Review visibility as part of trust/discovery cycle

### 5) Scale and Ecosystem Positioning

- 1 billion+ appointments booked (site claim)
- 130,000+ partner businesses (site claim)
- 450,000+ stylists/professionals (site claim)
- 120+ countries (site claim)

## Competitive Strengths (From Spectra Lens)

- Very strong marketplace-led customer acquisition model
- Global network effects (supply + demand flywheel)
- Consumer-grade discovery UX with high geographic coverage
- End-to-end journey from discovery to booking and post-visit feedback

## Opportunities for Spectra to Differentiate

- Offer deeper salon operations tooling visibility beyond discovery
- Differentiate on manager-first workflow intelligence and control
- Build tighter retention, rebooking, and margin optimization tooling
- Provide more explicit business-side analytics and performance actions
- Win on domain depth for salon operators versus broad marketplace breadth
