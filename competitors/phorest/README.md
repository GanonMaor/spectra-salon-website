# Phorest - Competitor Feature Research

## Company Snapshot

Phorest positions itself as an all-in-one salon, spa, and clinic management platform focused on scheduling, client retention, marketing performance, and multi-location operations.

## Core Product Areas

Phorest organizes its offering around:

1. Scheduling and client management
2. Payments and POS (PhorestPay)
3. Marketing and client growth
4. Team/location operations
5. Reporting and business control

## Full Feature Breakdown

### 1) Scheduling and Booking

- Central appointment calendar across channels (phone, website, social, branded app)
- 24/7 online booking widget
- Branded booking app for clients
- Intelligent scheduling (double-booking prevention, gap optimization)
- Waiting list handling for cancellations
- Deposit-supported booking to reduce no-shows
- Booking controls (staff availability, bookable services, cancellation notice windows)

### 2) Client Management and Communication

- Client CRM with history, notes, consultation forms, and image records
- Automated SMS and email confirmations/reminders
- Rebooking prompts and client reconnect workflows
- Digital consultation forms (including compliance-oriented forms)
- Self-check-in app
- Charting and treatment progress tracking
- Controls over what client data staff can access

### 3) Marketing, Reviews, and Retention

- Integrated marketing suite for email/SMS campaigns
- Campaign builder with templates and media assets
- Marketing ROI tracking tied to transactions
- Automated 5-star review collection flows (Google/Facebook)
- Loyalty program
- Referral rewards and tracking
- Phorest Ads Manager for Facebook/Instagram ad campaigns

### 4) Payments, Commerce, and Revenue Expansion

- PhorestPay integrated payment processing and POS
- Cardless checkout and integrated card terminals
- Cashless tipping
- Memberships
- Online store (retail between visits)
- Online gift vouchers
- Video consultations linked to retail conversion
- Expert Recommendations for product upsell flows

### 5) Team, Multi-Location, and Operations

- Multi-location support with branch-level visibility
- Chain library for linking staff, stock, rooms, services, and equipment per location
- Staff management (rosters, performance, commissions, permissions)
- Payroll and commission engine (flat/tiered models by role/service/product)
- Mobile management app (PhorestGo)
- Inventory and stock tools (barcode scanning, low-stock alerts, ordering)
- Scheduled and real-time reporting by location, staff, and date range

## Reported Business Impact Claims (From Feature Page)

- 69% fewer no-shows with deposits
- EUR14k additional quarterly revenue from SMS/email campaigns
- 38% increase in rebookings via branded booking app

## Competitive Strengths (From Spectra Lens)

- Strong retention and rebooking system depth
- Mature multi-location and team management capabilities
- End-to-end marketing-to-revenue attribution emphasis
- Rich consultation and compliance workflows for treatment-driven businesses
- Robust migration, training, and support positioning

## Opportunities for Spectra to Differentiate

- Deliver a simpler UX for day-to-day front-desk speed
- Build clearer AI-native workflows for managers and owners
- Offer stronger real-time predictive insights (churn risk, demand, staffing pressure)
- Reduce setup complexity for smaller teams while keeping enterprise depth
- Package decision support around measurable outcomes (time saved, rebooking lift, margin lift)
