# GlossGenius - Competitor Feature Research

## Company Snapshot

GlossGenius positions itself as an all-in-one business management platform for beauty and wellness businesses (including salons, spas, and medspas).

## Core Product Areas

GlossGenius groups its platform into five main areas:

1. Booking and Scheduling
2. Payments and Point of Sale
3. Core Operations
4. Client Relationships
5. Growth and AI

## Full Feature Breakdown

### 1) Booking and Scheduling

- Online booking without login or app download
- Custom website builder for booking pages
- Calendar and scheduling across multiple providers
- Resource management to prevent double booking
- Waitlist management for last-minute cancellations
- Reserve with Google integration
- No-show protection (deposits, cancellation policies, booking rules)

### 2) Payments and Point of Sale

- Payment processing with flat-rate pricing (2.6% stated on overview page)
- Card readers and POS support
- Tap to Pay on mobile devices
- Buy Now, Pay Later options
- Same-business-day transfers and instant payouts
- Memberships and packages
- Invoices
- Gift cards

### 3) Core Operations

- Inventory management (quantity tracking, barcode scanning, restocking workflows)
- Team and staff management with permissions
- Time tracking (clock-in/clock-out)
- Payroll synchronization (appointments, tips, commissions, taxes)
- Separate bank accounts support for booth renter models
- Finance tools (receipt upload, recurring costs, expense reporting)
- Reports and analytics dashboards
- Free data transfer/import from previous software

### 4) Client Relationships

- Detailed client profiles (history, photos, notes, formulas)
- Client management controls (policies, organization, restrictions)
- Automated client notifications (reminders, confirmations, birthday messages)
- Client insights (retention, referral sources, average client value)
- Automated review requests
- Forms and waivers in booking flow
- Marketing tools for email/SMS campaigns with AI assistance

### 5) Growth and AI

- AI Growth Analyst for business Q&A and recommendations
- AI tools across marketing, waitlist, and scheduling workflows
- Goal setting with benchmarks and real-time progress
- Financing options for business growth

## Competitive Strengths (From Spectra Lens)

- End-to-end all-in-one stack (booking + CRM + payments + operations + analytics)
- Strong automation for reducing no-shows and filling canceled slots
- Team-ready architecture (not limited to solo professionals)
- AI positioned as a practical revenue and efficiency accelerator
- Built-in growth channels (Google booking, review automation, targeted marketing)

## Opportunities for Spectra to Differentiate

- Offer deeper vertical-specific workflows for salon operations
- Build clearer ROI tooling tied to business outcomes per feature
- Provide faster setup and migration experiences for multi-location teams
- Add stronger decision support and predictive intelligence layers
- Improve transparency around pricing, feature limits, and upgrade paths
