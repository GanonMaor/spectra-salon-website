# Zenoti - Competitor Feature Research

## Company Snapshot

Zenoti positions itself as an AI-powered, all-in-one platform for beauty, wellness, and fitness brands, with strong emphasis on multi-location operations, automation, and enterprise scale.

## Core Product Areas

1. Guest experience and booking
2. Revenue growth automation
3. Staff productivity and payroll
4. Unified operations
5. AI-powered business acceleration

## Full Feature Breakdown

### 1) Guest Experience and Booking

- Online booking via website, app, Google, Instagram, and Facebook
- Kiosk and POS options
- Memberships and packages
- Loyalty programs
- Gift cards and referral programs
- AI receptionist for calls, booking, and FAQs

### 2) Growth and Revenue Automation

- Abandoned cart recovery
- Automated waitlist management
- Nearby location availability suggestions
- Preferred provider availability controls
- Automated upsell prompts
- Dynamic pricing and premium provider pricing
- Direct social booking paths

### 3) Staff Productivity and Payroll

- Unified appointment book syncing staff, rooms, and equipment
- Integrated payroll and tipping
- MyZen provider app for schedules, goals, and performance
- Mobile operations app for booking, payments, inventory, and reports

### 4) Unified Operations

- Integrated marketing
- Integrated payments
- Employee performance management
- Inventory management across locations
- Photo manager for before/after tracking

### 5) AI and Advanced Differentiators

- Zeenie AI assistant for questions, analysis, and insights
- HyperConnect engagement hub
- AI lead management
- Automated reputation management

## Scale Signals (From Official Platform Page)

- 30,000+ businesses on Zenoti
- Positioned as a platform for growing multi-location brands

## Competitive Strengths (From Spectra Lens)

- Very broad and deep all-in-one feature surface
- Strong AI coverage across operations, growth, and support
- Enterprise-ready model for scale and multi-location complexity
- Rich monetization levers beyond basic booking

## Opportunities for Spectra to Differentiate

- Offer a cleaner and simpler UX for small-to-mid salon teams
- Provide faster time-to-value with lower setup complexity
- Focus AI on practical daily workflows with clearer transparency
- Build superior operator guidance on profitability and staffing decisions
