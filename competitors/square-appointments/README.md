# Square Appointments - Competitor Feature Research

## Company Snapshot

Square Appointments positions itself as a full booking, POS, and operations platform for appointment-based businesses, combining scheduling, payments, staff tools, and ecosystem integrations.

## Core Product Areas

1. Scheduling and online booking
2. Payments and checkout
3. Client management and retention
4. Team operations and payroll support
5. Inventory and integrations

## Full Feature Breakdown

### 1) Scheduling and Booking

- Free 24/7 online booking website
- Automated SMS/email reminders
- Class booking and recurring sessions
- Google Calendar sync
- Waitlist management
- Multi-location and multi-time-zone support
- Booking widget integration (Wix, Squarespace, WordPress, etc.)
- Square Assistant for automated booking communications
- Instagram booking integration
- Resource booking (rooms, stations, chairs)
- No-show fee and cancellation policy controls

### 2) Payments and POS

- Integrated POS and secure payments
- Contactless and chip payments
- Card on file
- Tap to Pay on iPhone/Android
- Professional invoicing
- Next-day/same-day/instant transfer options
- Buy now, pay later via Afterpay
- Cash App Pay acceptance
- Gift cards (physical and digital)
- Offline payments and key-in payments

### 3) Client CRM and Retention

- Client profiles and notes
- Unified history for appointments, transactions, texts, and emails
- Automated campaigns through Square Marketing (add-on)
- Loyalty program support through Square Loyalty (add-on)
- File attachment support (forms/contracts/documents)
- SMS customization for reminder and booking flows

### 4) Team and Operations

- Individual employee accounts
- Granular permission sets and passcodes
- Timecards and labor-vs-sales reporting
- Multiple wage rates
- Commission tiers
- Team performance reports (retention, prebooking, utilization)
- Future booking report
- Staff and payroll integration via Square Payroll bundle

### 5) Retail, Inventory, and Ecosystem

- Combined service and product checkout
- Multilocation stock management
- Barcode scanning
- Low-stock alerts
- Sales and inventory reports
- App marketplace integrations (examples: Wix, QuickBooks, Jotform, Homebase)

## Competitive Strengths (From Spectra Lens)

- Highly complete scheduling-to-payment execution
- Strong operational tooling depth for teams
- Powerful integration ecosystem and modular expansion
- Mature inventory and reporting workflows

## Opportunities for Spectra to Differentiate

- Deliver salon-specific workflows with less platform sprawl
- Reduce complexity by simplifying cross-module setup
- Build clearer AI-native guidance for daily manager decisions
- Offer beauty-specific lifecycle automation out of the box
