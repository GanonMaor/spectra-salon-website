# Boulevard - Competitor Feature Research

## Company Snapshot

Boulevard positions itself as a client experience platform for appointment-based self-care businesses (salons, spas, med spas), with strong emphasis on modern UX, retention, and profitability.

## Core Product Areas

1. Scheduling and booking optimization
2. Payments and POS
3. Marketing and retention
4. Client profiles and service personalization
5. Reporting and multi-tool integration

## Full Feature Breakdown

### 1) Scheduling and Booking

- Custom online booking from business website
- Credit card capture at booking
- Suggested add-ons to increase ticket size
- AI-driven Precision Scheduling to optimize availability and revenue
- Centralized front desk workflows

### 2) Payments and POS

- Integrated POS and payments
- Refund and payout workflows
- Cardless checkout support
- End-to-end checkout integrated with booking and reporting

### 3) Marketing and Retention

- Automated campaigns
- One-time promotional blasts
- AI-powered marketing copy and subject line suggestions
- Offers, referrals, and loyalty programs
- Two-way texting and automated notifications

### 4) Client Experience and CRM

- Rich client profiles with forms, notes, history, and purchases
- Personalized visit workflows via tags, notes, and communication history
- Booking, communication, and loyalty in one connected journey

### 5) Reporting, Setup, and Integrations

- Customizable reporting for business performance and payroll insights
- Migration/onboarding support and ongoing training
- Public API and integrations for external systems

## Reported Business Impact Claims (From Boulevard Pages)

- 16% more services booked
- 71% fewer no-shows and late cancellations
- 18% more retail sales
- 24% more gratuity

## Competitive Strengths (From Spectra Lens)

- Strong booking optimization and retention positioning
- AI narrative tied to practical day-to-day workflows
- Balanced all-in-one stack for front desk, marketing, and payments
- Mature personalization layer for client experience

## Opportunities for Spectra to Differentiate

- Deliver clearer pricing transparency without demo-first friction
- Offer stronger operational intelligence for salon owners/managers
- Provide faster setup paths for small teams with minimal admin overhead
- Add deeper automation around staffing and profitability recommendations
